My Passion: Building PCs

I have a passion for Building an Customizing PCs. I was introduced to it by the age of 12 when I learned about Alienware Gaming PCs and other brands. And that it was better than if you built your own from scratch. This may sound funny but I can’t exactly pinpoint what inspired me to do this, but I can say I wanted to expand outside playing on consoles and try something new. Now I may have found an inspiration for PCs through the insane case mods people have created! It could be of an existing case or one that’s been homemade!
